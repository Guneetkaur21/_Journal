About this Journal

Online-Journals.org is a service provided by the non-profit International Association of Online Engineering. Its mission is to contribute to the advancement of science by providing an efficient and cost-effective way for making qualitative scientific results easily accessible to the largest possible audience. There are many categories of Journals :
- Academic/Scholarly Journals : To inform, report, and make available original research and new findings.
- Trade Journals : To report on industry trends, new products or techniques to people in a specific trade, business or profession.
- Current Affairs: To offer in-depth reporting and feature articles without scholarly conventions.
